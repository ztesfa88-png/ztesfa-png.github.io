About Wolkite University

Established in 2007, Wolkite University is one of Ethiopia's leading higher education institutions dedicated to academic excellence and societal transformation.

Wolkite University was established in 2007 by the Ministry of Education of Ethiopia with the mission to produce competent graduates who can contribute to the socio-economic development of the country.

The university is located in Wolkite town, Gurage Zone, Southern Nations, Nationalities, and Peoples' Region (SNNPR), approximately 158 kilometers south of Addis Ababa.

Today, Wolkite University has grown to include 8 colleges, 3 institutes, and 2 schools offering a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields of study.

Our vision is to become a center of excellence in teaching, research, and community service that contributes to national development and global knowledge advancement.
